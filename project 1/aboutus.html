Toto History & Establishment

Toto supermarket was established in Beirut, Lebanon in 2010. The supermarket first started as a fruits and vegetables market, then expanded to cans, and more and more to have bakery, butchery, and diary. Toto supermarket always gives the lowest prices compared to other supermarkets in Lebanon. Moreover, Toto supermarket gives the best quality products to its customers of large variety of cheese, bread, meat and chicken, fruits and vegetables. Since Toto supermarket started as a grocery, this means that we have the best quality of fruits and vegetables of good prices and fresh delivery daily.
